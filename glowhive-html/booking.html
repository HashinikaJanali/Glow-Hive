Consultation FAQs

What should I prepare for my consultation?

Come with clean skin (no makeup) and a list of any products you're currently using. It's helpful to note any skin concerns or goals beforehand.

How long does a consultation last?

Initial consultations typically last 45-60 minutes. Follow-up sessions are usually 30 minutes.

Can I do a virtual consultation?

Yes! We offer Zoom consultations with the same quality of service. You'll receive digital copies of all recommendations.

What's your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may incur a 50% fee.
